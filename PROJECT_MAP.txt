🔧 ALGORITHM FORMULA

Predicted Price = Current Price × (1 + Expected Return)

Where Expected Return = 
    (Sentiment Score × 0.25) +
    (Volume Ratio × 0.15) +
    ((1 - Volatility) × 0.20) +
    (News Frequency × 0.20) +
    (Analyst Rating × 0.20)

5 FACTORS ANALYZED:
  1️⃣ SENTIMENT (25%)        ← How positive/negative is the news?
  2️⃣ VOLUME (15%)           ← Trading volume vs average?
  3️⃣ VOLATILITY (20%)       ← How stable is the stock?
  4️⃣ NEWS FREQUENCY (20%)   ← How much media coverage?
  5️⃣ ANALYST RATING (20%)   ← Professional ratings?
